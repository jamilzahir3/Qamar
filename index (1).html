For My أمـيّي

If I could count the stars above,
Or gather the waves of the sea,
Still none would match the love
that endlessly lives in me.

As enchanting as skies that shine so wide,
As deep as the oceans at rising tide,
So is my heart, forever true,
Boundless in love, just for you. 💙✨

"أنتِ قصيدتي الأبدية، وصمتي المليء بالحُبّ."
